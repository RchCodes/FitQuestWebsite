FitQuest Account Deletion Instructions

If you wish to permanently delete your FitQuest account, please follow the instructions below according to your sign-in method.

1. Google Sign-In Users

  1. Open the Google Account Settings page.
  2. Go to Data & Privacy â†’ Apps and services connected to your account.
  3. Find FitQuest in the list of connected apps.
  4. Click Remove Access to disconnect FitQuest from your Google account.
  5. After revoking access, please email our support team at support@fitquest.com to request full account deletion.

2. Facebook Sign-In Users

  1. Open the Facebook App Settings page.
  2. Find FitQuest under the list of active apps.
  3. Click Remove App to revoke FitQuest access to your Facebook account.
  4. After revoking access, please email our support team at support@fitquest.com to request full account deletion.
Important: Once your account is deleted, all your game progress, rewards, and personal data will be permanently removed and cannot be recovered.
